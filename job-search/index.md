# Career Guide

## Getting Into the Job Hunting

### 4 Laws

1. The Fog Principle

- Things take time
- Go forward step by step

2. Perception VS Reality

- Perception becomes reality
- It's the perception we let other people see that will help get a job

3. Don't be a junior

- Never let people see us as a junior

4. Are you swimming with others ? (Do things that are unconventionnal)

- Don't follow the exact process as others

### Skill stacking

- Being very good at a few skills that can be combined

### A Mental Shift

1. You're not starting from 0

- Don't focus on technical skills alone

2. It's harder than you think

- It will take a lot of time (no shortcuts)

3. Think months and years

- Things take time, put in the time

4. 1000 failures is a winning strategy

- There is a 4% success rate for each interview, better fail than not trying
- The more we apply, the better chance we get a job

## Get More Interviews

1. Identify your career path

- The more we are clear about the job we want to do the easier it will be to get interviews

2. Invest in yourself

- Get/develop the necessary skills
- Education is the best investment

3. How to become a star

- Resume
- Portfolio
- LinkedIn
- Github
- Blog/Socials
