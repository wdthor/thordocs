### Origin

The origin is a combination of the **Protocol**, **Host**, and **Port** of a URL

- http://firt.dev
- https://firt.dev
- https://www.firt.dev
- https://firt.dev:3000

Are all different origins

Country TLDs, such as :

- .fr
- .us
- .uk

Are all different origins

Subdomains are also different origins

- https://firt.dev
- https://blog.firt.dev

To sum up :
Storage are origin based
