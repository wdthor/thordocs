# Opportunities and Network

## Getting Opportunities

- **Relationships Drive Opportunities** : You increase opportunities by building relationships of trust with those around you - Technical skills alone aren't enough : building trust-based relationships is essential for career growth
- **Collaboration and Respect** : Being difficult to work with or displaying microaggressions and dismissive behaviors can erode trust, limiting your opportunities regardless of your skill level
- **Trust Fuels Career Advancement** : The accumulation of opportunities leads to massive growth - Consistently good work and strong relationships lead to more opportunities and support from colleagues, accelerating your career growth

## Software Engineering is more than Code

- **Avoid the "Good Solutions Speak for Themselves" Trap** : It's easy to fall into the mindset that a great technical solution is all that matters. But it's crucial to validate that people actually want and need what you're building. Use empathy, care, and active listening to ensure you're solving the right problem
- **Focus on Impact** : Your effectiveness as a software engineer improves when you deliver work that truly impacts others and is actually used
- Consider Adoption : Delivering work doesn't stop at implementation. Think about how your solution will be adopted and used by others
